Legacy

Mega Man, known as "Rockman" in Japan, is one of Capcom's most iconic video game characters. First introduced in 1987 on the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES), Mega Man was created by Keiji Inafune and Akira Kitamura. The original game follows the story of a robot named Mega Man, built by Dr. Light to stop the evil plans of Dr. Wily, who reprograms robots for world domination. The game captivated audiences with its innovative gameplay and non-linear approach, which became a hallmark of the series. Over the decades, Mega Man evolved into a franchise spanning numerous sequels, spin-offs, and adaptations across various consoles, cementing its legacy as a cornerstone of Capcom's success in the video game industry.
